import {DoDontContainer, Do, Dont, Caption} from '@primer/doctocat-nextjs'

1. Import relevant components from `@primer/doctocat-nextjs`:

```js
import {DoDontContainer, Do, Dont} from '@primer/doctocat-nextjs'
```

2. Use the components in your Markdown file, but ensure that you wrap the component in a `div` with the `custom-component` class. This will ensure that article formatting isn't applied to the custom component.

```jsx
<DoDontContainer>
  <Do>
    <img src="https://via.placeholder.com/1000x350/d3d9df/d3d9df.png" />
    <Caption>Example of what you should do</Caption>
  </Do>
  <Dont>
    <img src="https://via.placeholder.com/1000x350/d3d9df/d3d9df.png" />
    <Caption>Example of what you shouldn't do</Caption>
  </Dont>
</DoDontContainer>
```

Will render:

<DoDontContainer>
  <Do>
    <img src="https://via.placeholder.com/1000x350/d3d9df/d3d9df.png" />
    <Caption>Example of what you should do</Caption>
  </Do>
  <Dont>
    <img src="https://via.placeholder.com/1000x350/d3d9df/d3d9df.png" />
    <Caption>Example of what you shouldn't do</Caption>
  </Dont>
</DoDontContainer>

### Stacked

Use the `stacked` option to vertically stack the Do and Don't images.

```jsx
<DoDontContainer stacked>
  <Do>
    <img src="https://via.placeholder.com/1000x350/d3d9df/d3d9df.png" />
    <Caption>Example of what you should do</Caption>
  </Do>
  <Dont>
    <img src="https://via.placeholder.com/1000x350/d3d9df/d3d9df.png" />
    <Caption>Example of what you shouldn't do</Caption>
  </Dont>
</DoDontContainer>
```
